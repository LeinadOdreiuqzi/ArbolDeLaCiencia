# Biology

Biology is the study of living organisms, from the tiniest bacteria to the largest whales. It helps us understand the diversity of life and the complex interactions within ecosystems.

## Main Topics
- **Cells:** The basic unit of life. [Learn more about cells](https://www.nature.com/scitable/topic/cell-biology-13906536/) (Nature Education)
- **Genetics:** The science of heredity and variation. [What is Genetics?](https://www.genome.gov/genetics-glossary/Genetics) (NIH)
- **Evolution:** The process by which species change over time. [Introduction to Evolution](https://evolution.berkeley.edu/evolibrary/article/evo_01) (Berkeley)

## Related Fields
- [[Chemistry]]: Explore the chemical foundations of life.
- [[Physics]]: Understand the physical principles that underlie biological processes.

## Why Study Biology?
Biology connects us to the natural world and provides the foundation for advances in medicine, agriculture, and environmental science. [Read more at Britannica](https://www.britannica.com/science/biology)
