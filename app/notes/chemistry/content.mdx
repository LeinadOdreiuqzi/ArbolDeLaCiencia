# Chemistry

Chemistry is the science of matter, its properties, and how substances interact, combine, and change. It plays a crucial role in everything from medicine to materials science.

## Main Topics
- **Atoms & Molecules:** The building blocks of matter. [What are atoms?](https://www.chemicool.com/elements/atom.html)
- **Reactions:** How substances transform. [Introduction to chemical reactions](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/chemistry/chemical-reactions-stoichiome)
- **Periodic Table:** Organizing all known elements. [Interactive Periodic Table](https://ptable.com/)

## Why Study Chemistry?
Chemistry helps us solve real-world problems, from developing new medicines to creating sustainable energy solutions. [Read more at ACS](https://www.acs.org/content/acs/en/careers/chemical-sciences/fields.html)
