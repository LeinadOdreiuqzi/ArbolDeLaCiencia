# Physics

Physics explores the fundamental laws of the universe, from the tiniest particles to the largest galaxies. It provides the foundation for understanding energy, motion, and the structure of matter.

## Main Topics
- **Motion & Forces:** The study of movement and interactions. [Basics of Motion](https://www.physicsclassroom.com/class/1DKin)
- **Quantum Physics:** The behavior of matter and energy at the smallest scales. [What is Quantum Physics?](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/qt-quantlog/)
- **Relativity:** How space and time are connected. [Einstein's Theory of Relativity](https://www.britannica.com/science/relativity)

- [[Biology]]: See how physics principles apply to living systems.

## Why Study Physics?
Physics drives technological innovation and helps us answer profound questions about the universe. [Read more at APS](https://www.aps.org/careers/physicists/index.cfm)
